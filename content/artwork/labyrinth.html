Source Energy Labyrinth was an installation Kristin Winn and I brought to Burning Man in 2022, supported by the Tautology Arts Collective. I was the lead engineer for the frame that allowed it to stay illuminated and welcoming to participants.

The full frame design and analysis of environmental force tolerances is available on Google Docs.
