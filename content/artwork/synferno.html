Synferno is a MIDI-enabled controller for sound stage flame effects, built and supported by the Tautology Arts Collective. I was its inventor and lead engineer, alongside playing a few DJ sets to break it in.

The full source code of the controller as well as electrical schematics are available on GitHub.
